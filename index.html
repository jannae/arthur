Meet Arthur. @ITPArthur

Tweet him. He does stuff. And then he tweets you back. Just like magic.

Arthur is a robot. The best Sparkfun box robot ever. And he really likes company. I promise. Most of all, he likes to keep people company when they're lonely.

But he can't work all by himself. Use the hash tags below when you tweet him (@ITPArthur) on this page to help Arthur keep his owner company!

#tweet

Get Arthur to send you a tweet!

#wave

Make him wave hello!

#love

Aww, get Arthur to show his love. <3

#dance

Happy dance time!

#blink

This just lets him know you're thinking of him. And he'll let you know he appreciates it.

#saycheese

Smile! You're on camera. Arthur cam, that is. :)

#help

This sends a tweet with a link to this page for more info on how to use Arthur.

So, you know, not so useful when you're *on* this page. :)
